General Assembly's Web Development Immersive class is a 12-week bootcamp that takes total programming noobs and remakes them into full-stack Ruby on Rails devs. After weeks of training, thousand of lines of code, and gallons of coffee, we're ready for the coding challenges of tomorrow.

THE CLASS

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, HAML, SASS, CoffeeScript, Ruby, Rails, and Ember.js are just some of the languages we know.

From day one we built as we learned and have collectively created dozens of unique projects both large and small.

From our past careers and the projects we accomplished together, we're team players and well versed in Git and Pivotal Tracker.

We learned core computer science fundamentals like data structures and algorithm processes to build a strong foundation.

FEATURED PROJECTS

Bobs and Bangs

Get the style you want, wherever you are. Bobs and Bangs is a hairstylist discovery app for stylists and clients.

By Tony Contreras,Eric Holland, Ron Jones & Sandip Trivedi

Barcode

Barcode is an app that enables bar owners to easily give rewards for user generated bar scavenger hunts.

By Jane Sternbach & Jillian Youngblood

Stylemine

A outfit tracking and planning app, Stylemine lets you save and share your outfits with your friends.

By Jillian Youngblood

Project3

Project3 is OKCupid for your career, matching you up with your next career move based on personality traits.

By Nick Blanchet & Bryan Wesley Reid

Get Reel

A movie storage system to help categorize your favorite films, Get Reel pulls data from IMDB and OMDB.

By Eric Holland

Eargasm

A music discovery app, Eargasm connects you to new music and connects you with concerts in your area.

By Simon Wong & Adi Singh
